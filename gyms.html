Our Gym Facilities

Take a look at the facilities and training spaces available at Iron Core Fitness.

Strength Zone

Our strength area is equipped with benches, squat racks, barbells, dumbbells, and resistance machines to support muscle building and power training.

Cardio Area

The cardio section includes treadmills, exercise bikes, rowing machines, and steppers for endurance and fat-burning workouts.

Locker Rooms

Clean and secure changing rooms with lockers are available for members before and after training sessions.

Group Class Space

We provide a spacious area for group exercise sessions, stretching, circuit workouts, and beginner-friendly fitness classes.

Personal Training Corner

Members working with trainers can use dedicated space for guided exercise sessions and body assessment support.

Recovery & Stretch Area

A quiet area for warm-up, cool-down, flexibility training, and recovery after demanding workouts.
